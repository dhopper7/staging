 Basics
Theming is based on the highly popular and open ThemeRoller, which is the standard for developing jQuery UI widgets. This means that you have access to 25 ready-to-use themes created by professional designers, plus a unique free web-based tool allowing you to create your own theme. You can also customize a theme starting from an existing one.
